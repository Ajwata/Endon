ENDON LEGAL DOCUMENTS

Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 — March 2026. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and what rights you have under GDPR.

1. Who We Are And How To Contact Us

Endon ("we", "us", "our") is the maker of the Endon emotional wellbeing app and acts as data controller for personal data processed through the app.

For privacy requests or complaints: customer@endonspace.com. You may also contact the Hellenic Data Protection Authority: www.dpa.gr.

2. What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to your use of the Endon app, the website at https://endonspace.com, and associated services. By using Endon, you confirm you have read and understood this policy.

3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Data You Give Us Directly

  • Account information: email, display name, date-of-birth confirmation, country, language preference, password.
  • Wellbeing content: conversations, journal entries, mood check-ins, exercise responses, reflections.
  • Feedback data: ratings, thumbs up/down signals, and voluntary feedback.
  • Support communications and subscription confirmation data from payment providers.

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Technical data: device type, OS, app version, IP address, device identifier.
  • Usage and performance data: session activity, navigation patterns, crash logs, error reports.
  • IP addresses are retained for up to 90 days, then anonymized.

4. Special Category Emotional Wellbeing Data

Conversations, journal entries, and mood data are special category personal data under GDPR Article 9. We process this data only with explicit consent to provide the Endon service.

  • Never sold to third parties.
  • Never used for advertising.
  • You can withdraw consent and delete data at any time.

5. Lawful Bases For Processing

  • Contract: delivering and operating the Endon service.
  • Explicit consent (Article 9(2)(a)): processing emotional wellbeing data.
  • Vital interests (Article 9(2)(c)): crisis safety situations.
  • Legitimate interests: security, personalization, and service improvement using de-identified data.
  • Legal obligation: required compliance processing.

6. Data Retention

  • Conversation content and journal entries: until deletion/account closure plus 7 days.
  • Mood check-ins and session metadata: up to 2 years.
  • Crisis flag records: up to 5 years.
  • Payment records: 7 years (Greek tax requirements).
  • Technical logs: 90 days, then anonymized.

7. AI And Automated Processing

Endon uses AI to personalize support, suggest relevant techniques, and detect distress patterns. As required by EU AI Act Article 50, you are informed that you are interacting with AI.

Automated processing does not make legally significant automated decisions under GDPR Article 22, and Endon maintains clinical oversight protocols.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data and do not share it with advertisers. Data is shared only with contracted service providers, where legally required, in emergency vital-interest cases, or in business transfer scenarios with equivalent safeguards.

9. International Transfers

Data is primarily stored and processed within the EU/EEA. If transfer outside the EU/EEA is required, we apply approved safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for conversations and journals.
  • AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • EU-based infrastructure, strict access controls, and regular security testing.

11. Data Breach Procedure

In a breach event, we assess and contain quickly, notify the supervisory authority where required, and notify affected users where high risk exists.

12. Your GDPR Rights

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection rights.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Hellenic DPA (www.dpa.gr).

To exercise rights: customer@endonspace.com.

13. Cookies

We use essential, functional, and analytics cookies to operate and improve Endon. You can manage preferences in-app and in your browser settings.

14. Children

Endon is intended for users aged 18 and over. If we learn data was provided by someone under 18, we delete it promptly.

15. Third-Party Service Providers

Current providers include Supabase (database/authentication), Stripe (payments), and F5-TTS (voice synthesis). All are contractually bound by Data Processing Agreements.

16. Changes To This Policy

For material changes, we provide at least 30 days notice in-app before changes take effect.

17. Contact

General support and privacy matters: customer@endonspace.com.
