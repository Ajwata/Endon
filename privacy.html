Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect your privacy.

What we collect

We collect account data, app usage data, and the content you provide such as conversations, mood logs, and journal entries.

How we use your data

  • To provide and improve Endon features.
  • To personalize support based on your usage.
  • To maintain security and service reliability.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data protection

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored on EU infrastructure, and handled under GDPR requirements.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data. You can also withdraw consent and close your account.
